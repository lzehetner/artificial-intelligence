Introduction

Artifical Intelligence or AI is the simulation of human thought and behaviour by computers and machine systems. It is used to solve complex tasks normally done by humans, such as pattern and trend recognition, decision making and translation. In recent years, AI technology has developed rapidly and is being used in more and more fields, such as medicine, finance and even the entertainment industry. However, it is also a controversial topic as it raises questions regarding ethics and jobs
